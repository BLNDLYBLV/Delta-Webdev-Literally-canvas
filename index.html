Literally a canvas
This is the pencil tool. It is used to freedraw inside the canvas.
This is the text tool. It is used put a text inside of the canvas
This is the eraser tool. It is used to erase the content on the canvas. Think of it as an white pencil. And it has bit more width than pencil tool as i find it annoying for an eraser tool to be small
This is the shapes tool. It is used to draw regular polygons when it is specified with no of sides and side's length. When provided the specifics if you click on the canvas a regular polygon is stamped with center as the click point. Pro tip if want a background of some color choose the color and give great side length like 100 and tick the fill box, and just stamp on the canvas to get the background !
This is the line tool. It is used to draw straight lines. Just click at a point (p1) drag and release at point (p2), There will be a line b/w p1 and p2
This is the circle tool. If you specify the radius and click on the canvas a circle will be formed with center as the click point.
This is your current chosen color. It is always updated when you choose a color
This is the color pallete. You can choose from the different predefined colors provided by clicking the color
This is the color chooser. If you are not satisfied with the colors provided in thecolor pallete you can choose any color in the world here !
This is the line width chooser. You can choose the width of your lines from the four options provided from 1 being the thinnest to 4 being the largest
This is the fill checkbox. If it is ticked the shape formed will be filled with the current color chosen
And if the accuracy is not correct just scroll and try again
